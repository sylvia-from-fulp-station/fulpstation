remove_relevant_organs
(/mob/living/carbon/target)

'purrbation_apply()' removes the external spines on mobs that it hits, but spines are just one external organ of many that we "need" to remove prior to giving our target cat organs.

This proc properly removes all relevant external organs from our cateor's target using 'mob_remove()' rather than 'Remove()', which is used by 'purrbation_apply()'.
